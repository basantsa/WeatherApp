Weather App

Weather, everybody wants to know how it is going to be during the week. Will it be rainy, windy, or sunny? This app helps in bookmarking your favourite cities and check the weather there with just a click of a button.

Just click on the "+" icon on the top right hand corner which would take you to the map, and long press on the map to drop a pin and click done to bookmark that city.

To delete a bookmarked city, just swipe the city and click on delete.


Weather info provided by: http://openweathermap.org
